ENGINEERING SERVICES

PPG is providing Engineering Services and we are moving ahead with customers requirement on priority basis. We are well trained and well equipped to provide all sorts of Services in Power Gen field.

Our workshop setup has provisions for the following:

  • Portable generator services
  • Dismantling & assembling/overhauling of gensets
  • Servicing of alternator
  • Load test of gensets
  • Refurbishing engine control wiring
  • Other engineering services are outsourced by our reliable suppliers and vendors
PORTABLE GENSETS SERVICES
  • For household generators we provide affordable and reliable repair and services at our equipped workshop
EMERGENCY REPAIRS (Electrical/Mechanical)
  • Our trained team is in stand by to serve our valued customers economically on all emergency repairs
TOP/MAJOR OVERHAULING
  • We do Top & Major Overhauling of Gensets in customer premises and in our Workshop according to manufacturers’ standards with standard warranty period
CALIBRATION OF FUEL PUMP & INJECTORS
  • PPG provides calibration services of pump and injectors according to the OEMs’ standards
OPERATION & MAINTENANCE
  • PPG provides O & M of powerhouse/plant site/…our team would inspect your equipments, study the technical specifications provided by the OEMs, maintains & records the data and take preventive measures for safety purposes as described by the OEMs
ALTERNATOR REPAIRS
  • Services and re-varnishing of Alternators
  • Rotor
  • Stator
  • Excitor windings
INSTALLATION / COMMISSIONING
  • PPG team will work for installation and commissioning at your site without interruption in your business with proper wiring and electrical works ensuring safety all the time. We arrange all the delivery, rigging and transportation; if required by the customer and provides all the mechanical and electrical works required at your site.
ANNUAL MAINTENANCE CONTRACTS
  • For regular preventive maintenance of diesel Gensets by our trained and professional team, through AMC we provide you monthly/bi-monthly services for your Gensets so you can run your Gensets trouble free throughout the year.
CANOPY FABRICATION
  • Sound proof canopy fabrication We provide a wide range of Gensets Canopy fabrications for old and new generators for various engines and makes; meeting all the customers’ requirement for timely delivery, quality and support
RENTAL SOLUTIONS
  • Progressive Power Generators is a reliable rental power provider, we understand urgency of your needs.
RADIATOR SUPPLY/REPAIR
  • Industrial and Remote Radiator Package systems, along with repair and installation services,Just give us the opportunity.
AUTO MAIN FAILURE PANEL (AMF)
  • We provide AMF panel to check status of generator and Mains supplies for protecting of your Generator from over load and short circuit protection.
AUTO TRANSFER SWITCH (ATS)
  • Portable/small generator transfer switch and industrial/large generator transfer switch
  • We offer Auto Transfer Switch installation; if you are unaware of your ATS type & size PPG can guide you by our qualified and trained engineers they consider the voltage and amperage of the electrical loads you want to run.
  • We also provide engineering design and prototyping services for monitoring, control and protection panels according to customers’ requirement
  • We offer system improvement (its efficiency & reliability) by providing our re-engineering services to already commissioned panels/systems.
ECM CALIBRATION
  • Calibration of Cummins, Cat, Perkins, Volvo, others…
BI-FUEL KITS

SYNCHRONIZING PANELS

  • Enhance your fire or power engines with our advanced retrofit solutions, improving fuel efficiency, emissions compliance, and operational longevity
INDUSTRIAL / CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENTS
