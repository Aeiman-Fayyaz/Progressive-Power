INFORMATION ABOUT PROGRESSIVE POWER

PPG is a Power Engineering Services Company (Cummins/ CAT & Power GenTeam), Registered with SECP with a Mission to provide complete Sales and Service solution to our Valued Customers segment wise from Home, Commercial to Industries, Power Plants, Hospitals, Hotels, Telecom, Banking Sectors, Cement Sector, Marine, Automotive, Industrial, Oil & Gas Sector, Locomotive etc.

We deal in Sales, Parts and After Market Support of new & refurbished equipment’s supported by well-equipped workshop, special tools, warehouse facilities and our dedicated & trained Cummins team all over Pakistan.

PPG offer wide range of Power Generation products & All Industrial / Construction products Power pacs / Fire Pacs / Pumps / Earth Moving equipment HP / KW & Gensets ratings from 10KVA to 2000KVA European / USA/China / Far-East Used / New Brands etc, with variety, economical prices and covered under limited warranty.

Also, PPG offer other equipment’s as per customer’s requirements, like:

Sound & weather proof Canopy
Auto Main Failure Panel (AMF)
Auto Transfer Switch (ATS)
Synchronizing panels
Switch Gears
Bi-fuel Kits
Electronic Control Modules (ECM) calibration Cummins, CAT, Perkins, Volvo & others…

PPG provide services round the clock to our valued customers, targeting 100% customer satisfaction.
